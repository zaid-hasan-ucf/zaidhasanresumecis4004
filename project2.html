Project 2: Home Network Revamp

Description:

In this project, I overhauled my home network security system by seperating my wireless networks and segmenting them into different VLANs. For example, primary household wifi, guest wifi, and IoT devices were all segmented. Through this process, I implemented different traffic flow rules across these VLANs and also introduced mDNS reflection for AirPrint compatibiltiy across seperate networks.

Skills:

I learned more about managing a home network along with best practice rules for securing/hardening a network and ensuring proper auditing/logging of events. I also gained a better understanding of different communication network ports and what services they are commonly used by.
